Diferences Between Halo on OG Xbox and on PC

The Year is 2002, Halo has been the Xbox's Killer app but Microsoft also has another platform, Windows. Microsoft realised that putting Halo on PC would make Windows the Ultimate Computer. Windows XP was stable and fast and it would have the best game of the year Microsoft Game Studios contracted GearBox Studios to Port Halo Combat Evolved to PC (And then later Mac)

Halo For PC launched in 2003 to many great reviews but something just seemed wrong. The World felt unalive and the walls just felt flat. Gearbox had seriously messed somthing up. Halo used something called bump-maps to make the world seem 3D. They used light and relfections to create the illusion of depth. Gearbox somehow managed to break the lighting in the port and as a result the Bump-maps do no longer work. You can see how they were meant to look by shining a torch on it. And On Energy Sheilds Bullets would bounce off them or how warthogs would rip up the ground
